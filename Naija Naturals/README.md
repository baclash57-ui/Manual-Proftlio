NN Hair & Beauty is a local e-commerce store that sells hair products, mostly for women. They offer both in-store and door-to-door delivery options for users and allow them to compare products. They serve as Nigeria's leading beauty store with a primary focus on hair care products and equipment.

---

**Core User Flow**

1. Browsing products on the homepage and product category pages
2. Searching for products by name, brand, and description (e.g., hair spray)
3. Filtering items on the product listing page to the preferred type
4. Viewing the Product Description Page
5. Adding items to cart
6. Payment for items
7. Tracking orders

---

**In-Scope Areas**

1. **User Navigation:** From the homepage to the checkout section. Payments are out of scope.
2. **Homepage:**
    - Check the functionality of the menus, sub-menus, and banner images, and ensure that the user is redirected to the correct page
    - Verify the arrangement of elements (images, banner images, and texts) on the homepage
    - Verify the responsiveness of the homepage when a button is clicked
    - Validate that clicking the company logo redirects the user back to the homepage
3. **Search & Filter:**
    - Search for products by name, brand, description, and category
    - Review the Search Results Page and verify that results match what was searched for
    - Search for products within a specific category (e.g., search for a type of spray within the Best Sellers category)
    - Search for different products within a particular category
    
    **Filter:**
    
    - Select filter options on the SRP and homepage sections
    - Adjust the price range and observe the page behaviour
    - Add and remove filters and observe how the site behaves
    - Refresh the page and verify whether filters persist
    - Apply multiple filters simultaneously and observe site behaviour
4. **Product Description Page:**
    - Verify that tapping on a product image navigates the user to the PDP
    - Verify that the content on the PDP matches the product selected
    - Verify that the user can change product options where available
    - Verify that the user can view all images of a product on the PDP when multiple images are present
5. **Cart Section:**
    - Verify that the user can add items to the cart from the PDP, SRP, and Product Recommendation sections
    - Verify that the cart count matches the total number of items in the cart
    - Verify that the subtotal matches the actual price of all items in the cart
    - Verify that the user can add and remove items from the cart
    - Verify cart behavior when multiple items are present
    - Verify that the user can proceed to the checkout page
