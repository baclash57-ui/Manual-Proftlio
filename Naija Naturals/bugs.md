**Bug ID: BG-NN_001**

**Title:** Nationwide Fast Delivery Button Redirects User to the FAQ Page

**Type:** Functional

**Priority:** Medium

**Environment:** Windows 11

**Browser:** Microsoft Edge

**Summary:** When a user clicks on the Nationwide Fast Delivery button located below the NN Naturals logo, they are incorrectly redirected to the FAQ page instead of the expected destination page.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Open https://naijanaturals.com/
2. Click on any menu or sub-menu item on the homepage
3. Click on the Nationwide Fast Delivery button located below the NN Naturals logo

**Expected Result:** The user should be redirected to a page related to the Nationwide Fast Delivery service, such as a delivery information or shipping policy page

**Actual Result:** The user is incorrectly redirected to the FAQ page instead of the expected delivery-related page

**BugID: BG-NN_002**

**Title:** Search Results Page Returns Unrelated Items

**Type:** Functional

**Priority:** High

**Environment:** Windows 11

**Browser:** Microsoft Edge

**Summary:** When a user searches for a specific product, the Search Results Page returns items that are unrelated to the search query entered. This affects the accuracy of the search functionality and may cause users to lose confidence in the platform.

**Steps to Reproduce:**

1. Open https://naijanaturals.com/
2. Click on the search bar
3. Type "Hair Dye" and press Enter or click the search icon
4. On the SRP, observe all the products returned
5. Click on the fourth product and verify its details
6. Navigate back to the SRP and click on the last product and verify its details

**Expected Result:**

 All products displayed on the SRP should be hair dye products or items directly related to hair dye (e.g., developer, applicator brush, gloves). No unrelated products should appear in the results

**Actual Result:** 

The SRP displays items that are unrelated to hair dye alongside the items searched for
