**ID:** BG-PLP_001

**Title:** Candy items with different colors are displayed when searching for candy by a particular color

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Functional |
| **Severity** | Low |
| **Device** | ASUS Windows 11 Pro |
| **Browser** | Microsoft Edge, Google Chrome |

---

**Summary**

There are other colored candy items displayed in the PLP when user searches for a specific color.

---

**Steps to Reproduce**

1. Open https://www.candystore.com/
2. Scroll down to the "Find the Perfect Candies for Your Themed Event Here!" text
3. Click on select by color
4. Click on green candy, This will take you to a new page displaying only candy with green color
5. On the Product Listing page, move from page one to page two and then to page three
6. observe the candy in each page

---

**Expected Result**

All the candy displayed are green in colour.

---

**Actual Result**

There are some candy items in another color(blue) displayed.
