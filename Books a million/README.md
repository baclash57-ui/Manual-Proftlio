### Overview

Books-A-Million is an e-commerce website that allows users to browse, search for, and purchase books across multiple categories and formats. Users can view product details, add books to their cart, complete purchases, and interact with customer support features such as live chat.

---

### Core User Flows

1. User authentication and navigation
    - Logging in or signing up
    - Navigating from the homepage to other sections such as product pages and cart
2. Browsing and discovery
    - Viewing books on the homepage
    - Browsing books by category or genre
3. Search functionality
    - Searching for books by title, author, or category
    - Viewing relevant search results
4. Product selection
    - Selecting a book from search results or listings
    - Viewing product details on the product description page
5. Cart management
    - Adding books to the cart
    - Removing books from the cart
    - Verifying price calculations
6. Customer support
    - Interacting with the live chat feature to get assistance

---

### In-Scope Areas

1. Homepage
    - Functionality of homepage elements
    - Proper arrangement and display of images and text
    - Verification of functional links
2. Search
    - Searching for books by title, author, genre, and category
    - Ensuring search results match the user’s input
3. Filter Options
    - Applying filters after a search
    - Removing filters and verifying updated results
4. Product Description Page
    - Verifying the selected book’s details match the product clicked
    - Checking accuracy of title, author, format, and pricing
5. Cart
    - Adding and removing books from the cart
    - Verifying item count and subtotal calculations

---

### Out-of-Scope Areas

1. Book reviews and review search functionality
2. Social media links and integrations
3. External company information and help pages in the footer
4. Features not directly related to browsing, searching, or purchasing books
5. Toys & Games
6. Millionaire’s  Club
