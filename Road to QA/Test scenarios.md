**Test Scenarios  (CRUD-Methodology)**

---

**Homepage Navigation Links**

**Read (Navigation & Content)**

**Valid Scenarios**

Verify that all homepage links redirect users to the correct pages.

Verify that links are clickable and load within acceptable time.

Verify that navigation links work correctly across supported browsers and devices.

**Invalid Scenarios**

Verify that broken or incorrect links are not present on the homepage.

Verify that users are shown an error or fallback message if a linked page is unavailable.

**User Stories Section (Videos and Images)**

---

**Read (Media Content)**

**Valid Scenarios**

Verify that videos in the user stories section load and play successfully.

Verify that images display clearly without distortion or overlap.

Verify that media content is consistent across different screen sizes.

**Invalid Scenarios**

Verify that the system handles missing or unavailable media gracefully.

Verify that broken images or videos do not crash the page.

Verify that users receive appropriate feedback if media fails to load.

---

**Reviews and User Success Stories**

**Read (Reviews)**

**Valid Scenarios**

Verify that users can view reviews and success stories without enrolling.

Verify that reviews display correct user information and content.

Verify that reviews are displayed in the intended order.

**Invalid Scenarios**

Verify that incomplete or corrupted review data is not displayed.
